-- Safety Brief --
SEVERITY: MEDIUM

1) SUMMARY
- GHS Code: GHS07 (Harmful) & GHS03 (Oxidizer) & GHS02 (Flammable)
- Severity: Medium

2) HAZARDS
  - Harmful if swallowed
  - Harmful in contact with skin
  - Causes serious eye irritation
  - May cause respiratory irritation
  - Oxidizer — may cause or intensify fire

3) PPE REQUIRED
  - Wear protective gloves
  - Avoid breathing dust, fumes, gas, mist, vapors, or spray
  - Wear eye protection
  - Wear fire-resistant clothing
  - Wear chemical splash goggles

4) HANDLING SOP
  1. Do not swallow
  2. Avoid skin contact
  3. Use only outdoors or in a well-ventilated area
  4. Wash hands thoroughly after handling
  5. Keep away from combustible materials (clothing, wood, paper, oil)
  6. Take precautionary measures against static discharge

5) STORAGE
  - Store in a well-ventilated place
  - Keep container tightly closed
  - Store away from food, beverages, and animal feed
  - Store oxidizers separately from other hazardous chemicals

6) EMERGENCY / FIRST AID
  - If swallowed: immediately call Poison Control; rinse mouth; do NOT induce vomiting
  - If skin contact: wash with soap and water; call a doctor if irritation persists
  - If inhaled: move to fresh air; call a doctor if you feel unwell
  - In case of fire: use water spray or foam — oxidizer may intensify fire

7) AUTONOMOUS SAFETY ACTIONS
  ACTION: Set flammable handling mode — remove all ignition sources from area
  ACTION: Require chemical-resistant gloves before handling — verify availability
