  - Causes severe skin burns, eye damage, or corrodes metals

  - May be corrosive to metals
  - Irreversible tissue destruction on contact
  - Do not breathe mist, vapors, or spray

3) PPE REQUIRED

  - Use full-face shield or chemical splash goggles
  - Wear acid/alkali-resistant apron or suit
  - Use respiratory protection if vapor or mist is generated

4) HANDLING SOP
  1. Do not breathe mist, vapors, or spray
  2. Wash hands and exposed skin thoroughly after handling
  3. Do not eat, drink, or smoke when using this product
  4. Use only in well-ventilated areas

5) STORAGE
  - Store in corrosion-resistant container with a resistant inner liner
  - Keep tightly closed and upright to prevent leakage
  - Separate from metals and incompatible chemicals

  - If eye contact: rinse cautiously with water for at least 15 minutes; remove contact lenses if easy; seek immediate medical attention
